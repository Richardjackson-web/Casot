About Us

CASOTs primary core existence is to unrelentingly help change the experience and narrative obtainable for tertiary institution students right from their entry into the school system through her online platform which allows students to make the most out of their stay on campus such that upon graduation, they would have created an impactful success story that cuts across academic excellence, personal developments and financial independence for themselves.

We are currently focussed on providing a platform that will help students, especially those in 100 level to practise tons of test/exam possible/past questions unique to their schools with a detailed and easy to understand solution on challenging courses.

CASOTs is committed to adding advanced features, products/services that will certainly help studens at all levels earn n, save cost, save time and learn.
